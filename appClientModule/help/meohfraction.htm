Methanol Fraction control

Move the slider or enter a number in the text box to select the ratio of methanol to water (in volume percent) in the eluent.

Remarks

Lower methanol content (higher water content) makes the eluent more polar, causing compounds to be retained longer on the non-polar stationary phase. In the same way, higher methanol content makes the eluent less polar, reducing the time compounds are retained in the column.
