Eluent Viscosity indicator

Indicates the viscosity of the eluent (in cP) at the given solvent composition and temperature. In gradient mode, the eluent viscosity indicator only shows "--". To see the eluent viscosity, you must plot it by choosing "Plot mobile phase viscosity" in the "Plot Options" control panel.
